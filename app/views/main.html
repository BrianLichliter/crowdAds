How it works

Find a contest

Brands and companies are posting new contests everyday. They pick out a hashtag, set a prize, and post a description of what they want to see!.

Make a video

This is the fun part. Go out a make a video or clip that relates to what the company wants to see! Grab a friend and just have fun with it, you might just have what it takes to win.

Post the video

We use the amazing microclip platform vine as a landing spot for video submissions. Just post your video on vine with the right hashtag and you'll be entered to win the prize for the contest.
